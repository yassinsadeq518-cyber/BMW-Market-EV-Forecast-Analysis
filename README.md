# BMW-Market-EV-Forecast-Analysis
# 🚗 BMW Global Sales & EV Transition Forecast (2018-2030)

## 📌 Project Overview
An advanced business intelligence project analyzing BMW's historical sales performance and forecasting its transition towards Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) up to the year 2030, considering macroeconomic indicators like fuel prices and GDP.

## 📊 Key Insights & Features
* **Revenue Drivers:** Identified the X7 and iX models as the primary global revenue generators.
* **EV Adoption Dynamics:** Uncovered a strong correlation between rising global fuel prices and an increased BEV market share (surging up to 19%).
* **Predictive Forecasting:** Built time-series forecasting models showing steady sales volume expansion in the European and Chinese markets towards 2030.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **DAX Formulas:** Developed time-intelligence and conditional forecasting measures.
* **Macroeconomic Integration:** Correlation analysis between market sales and external economic factors.
